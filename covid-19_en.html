Report to the university

Tottori University published action you are supposed to take on 2020.03.31.

If you have cold symptos or fever

For the time being, the university does not count your class absence if you report the absence as well as submit recovery applications.

If you (students) have cold symptos or fever

  1. Don't go to school until you recover
  2. stay home or visit medical institution
  3. report your absence to the student affairs section of your faculty via email
